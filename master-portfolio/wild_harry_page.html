HARRY POTTER IN THE WILD WEST

"Harry Potter in The Wild West" is a group project Created during KEA Beasic Animation theme. It's inspired by battle scene of Harry and Malfoy from the movie "Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets."

We tried to recreate the scene as much as possible, however decided to place the characters in completely new universe. In order to present make sure our audience will recognize their favourite movie characers, we kept main attributes such as cloakes, Harry's glasses or the scar. We also worked within original Gryffindor's and Sliterin's colour shemes.

However, we also applied some major changes. In order to fit characters more into western theme we replaced their wands with guns and their wizard hats with cowboy hats instead.
