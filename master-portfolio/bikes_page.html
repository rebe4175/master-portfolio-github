BICYCLE PROGRAM PROMO VIDEO

KØBENHAVNS KOMMUNE

As Copenhagen citizens, we all know how dangerous it is to bike around the city. Unfortunately, there are plenty of people stating their bike adventures every day having no idea how to ride safely around Copenhagen.

Bicycle program promo is a project with an informative purpose created for Københavns Kommune. It's intented for students coming from abroad that are just about to begin their school programs in Copenhagen. In order to appeal to our targert audience we have chosen young actors and short, dynamic plot to keep their attention.
