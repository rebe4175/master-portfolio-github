CITY OF LONELINESS

City of loneliness is a video project consisting of a story without words. Among tree general themes: "Urban living", "People who are too busy" and "Difcult liaisons" we focused on a problem of loneliness as a conseguence of living in a crowded city where everyone is hurrying somewhere. We found it as an appealng topic for all age groups and put emphasis on the fact, that we all sometimes experience being lost and overwhelmed with big city lifestyle.
